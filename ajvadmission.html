ADMISSIONS PROCEDURE

  • 1) Xerox copy of Birth Certificate (Original to be produced at the time of admission for verification).
  • 2) Two / Three copies of recent passport size photographs.
  • 3) Transfer Certificate along with the marksheet of the last examination appeared (for students coming from other schools).
  • 4) Two copies of identity & address proof of parents (Xerox).
  • 5) Two passport size photographs of parents.
  • 6) Medical report of the child.
  • 7) Xerox copy of Aadhaar Card of the child and both parents.
